About Me

I grew up in Eagle River, Alaska, of all places. When I graduated from high school I went straight to th local university for music education, thinking that since I was the band council president, a teaching career would be great. Spoiler alert: it wasn't all that cracked up to be. So! I did somethign I never thought I'd ever do in my life: I dropped out. And it was liberating! But pretty boring after a couple months. School had been a staple of my life since I was five, and here I was with an enormous void. So I traveled for a couple years, grew up, moved to Anchorage, taught myself Ruby out of boredom, and decided to get in on the tech boom.
